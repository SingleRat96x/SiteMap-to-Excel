Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2024

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how the Sitemap to XLSX Extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles your information.

Information Collection

The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. It only processes:

  • Website URLs you manually enter for sitemap extraction
  • Sitemap data from the websites you choose to analyze

All processing is done locally in your browser, and no data is sent to external servers except for fetching sitemaps from the URLs you provide.

Data Storage

The extension temporarily stores:

  • Your most recent sitemap URL
  • Extracted URLs from the sitemap
  • Filter settings you apply

This data is stored locally in your browser and is cleared when you use the reset function or uninstall the extension.

Future Updates and Advertising

Please note that future versions of this extension may include advertising features to support ongoing development and maintenance. If implemented, ads will:

  • Be clearly labeled as advertising content
  • Not interfere with the core functionality of the extension
  • Follow all applicable privacy laws and Google's advertising policies
  • Not collect personal information without explicit consent
  • Include options to manage ad preferences

Users will be notified of any significant changes to the privacy policy or the introduction of advertising features through extension updates.

Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All network requests use HTTPS
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) to prevent unauthorized code execution
  • Local data storage with browser security controls

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all stored data using the reset function
  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through extension updates.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support channel.
